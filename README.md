## 🎵 About Aura
**Aura** is a beautifully designed, minimalist radio player extension for Google Chrome. Built with a modern dark-neon aesthetic, Aura allows you to listen to your favorite radio stations, manage your own streams, and set smart audio schedules—all without keeping a heavy website open in your tabs.

### ✨ Key Features
* **Custom Stations:** Add any streaming URL to build your personal radio library.
* **Smart Schedules & Alarms:** Wake up to your favorite show. Set specific times for a station to start playing, or enable **Autostart on Browser Launch**.
* **Sleep Timer:** Fall asleep to music. Set a timer and the radio will automatically stop.
* **Now Playing Metadata:** See the current artist and song title right in the extension popup and via non-intrusive Chrome notifications.
* **Import & Export:** Never lose your stations. Backup your library and import it on any other device.
* **Lightweight:** Built on Manifest V3 using modern Web Audio APIs. Minimal CPU and RAM usage.

## 🔒 Privacy Policy
Aura is designed to respect your data. 
- All your saved stations, schedules, and settings are stored **locally** on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. 
- We do not track your browsing history, nor do we collect or sell your personal data. 
- The extension only connects to the internet to fetch the audio streams of the radio stations you choose to play.
